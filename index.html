About Me

Hi there! I am a first year MSCS student at the Georgia Institute of Technology, specializing in Visual Analytics. I previously completed my BCS at the University of Waterloo in 2024.

Research Interests

I am passionate about exploring data visualization and computer graphics, with a particular interest in applying visualization tools and 3D graphics to biological datasets. I am also interested in the intersection of technology and education, exploring how these tools can be leveraged to enhance learning experiences. I aspire to create intuitive user interfaces that make scientific data more accessible and engaging.

In my free time, I'm a big fan of knitting, soccer, and musical theatre!
